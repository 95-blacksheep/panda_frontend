HEADQUARTERS

RE - APPLICATION FOR PREMATURE RETIREMENT BY
POLICE WOMAN CHIEF INSPECTOR JANET JACKSON

Your Letter NO. PH/04/PZ/60 dated 5th April, 2024 on the above subject refers

I am directed by the Inspector-General of Police to inform you that approval has been given for you to retirement prematurely from the Service with immediate effect.
2. Any leave not enjoyed before the effective date of your retirement shall be FORFIETED.
3. You are to ensure that all Police accoutrements in your possession are handed over with immediate effect to the officer under whom you are serving before you proceed on retirement.
4. You are to indicate in due course where you wish to draw your retiring awards in order to facilitate the submission of your pension papers to the Accountant General's Department.
Also, you are to leave a forwarding address with this Headquarters before your date of retirement.
5. You are reminded to contact the Director Pensions, Police Headquarters/nearest Finance Office with the following:-

I. Current pay slip
II. 5 passport-size photographs
III. Evidence of surrender of accoutrement

6. Your attention is drawn to the provisions of Regulation 128 (1) of the Police Service Regulations, 2012 (C. I. 76) regarding accomodation in the Service. You will accordingly enjoy 90 days grace from the date of retirement and vacate your quarters immediately after that (i.e. if you live in Police accomodation). You are to ensure that inventory is taken of all items on charge at your quarters before you proceed on leave pending retirement. Copy of inventory taken must be logged with the Regional Commander.
7. On occasion of your retirement, the Police Administration wishes you the best in your future endeavours and takes this opportunity to thank you for your invaluable services to the Police Service.
8. Acknowledge receipt of this letter through the officer under whom you are serving.

This letter superscedes Headquarters letter number PN.12345/23 dated 19-02-2024.

Christian Tetteh Yohunu (Mr.)
Commissioner of Police
Director-General/Administration
